Welcome to Tokyo Mavericks. This app helps our team plan training sessions, RSVPs, and carpools around Tokyo.

You sign in with email and password. The home dashboard greets you and highlights the next session, RSVP status, and any deadlines closing soon.

The Sessions page lists upcoming and past training. Each card shows status: open for RSVP, closed, optimised for carpooling, or published when rides are final.

Opening a session shows the venue on a map, the RSVP deadline, and who is coming. You can say whether you are attending, need a ride, can drive with pickup details, or travel on your own with public transport or walking estimates.

After admins optimise and publish carpools, drivers and riders see their assignments, pickup points, and a map of routes.

Stats summarises travel times for you and the team with charts, so we can see commute patterns over time.

Attendance and rides lists every session roster: who is in, and how each person travels. Admins can adjust details when plans change.

The Help page explains the full workflow in one place.

Administrators create new sessions with venue location and RSVP deadlines.

On Carpool assignments, admins close RSVP when ready, run optimisation using travel matrices, tweak groups if needed, and publish so everyone sees their ride.

Finally, user management lets admins add or update team accounts.

That is Tokyo Mavericks: sessions, RSVPs, smart carpools, stats, attendance, and admin tools in one place. Thanks for watching.
